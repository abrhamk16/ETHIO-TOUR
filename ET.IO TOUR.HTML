Culture, Events & Celebrities

Celebrities

Abebe Bikila: The first African to win Olympic gold (marathon).

Haile Selassie I: The defining Emperor of modern Ethiopia.

Derartu Tulu: Trailblazing female long-distance champion.

Cultures

Coffee Ceremony: A social ritual of roasting and brewing.

Eskista: Traditional shoulder-dancing unique to Ethiopia.

Injera: The staple sourdough flatbread shared in 'Agelgil'.

Events

Timkat: The Epiphany celebration with colorful processions.

Meskel: The finding of the True Cross with giant bonfires.

Enkutatash: The vibrant Ethiopian New Year in September.
