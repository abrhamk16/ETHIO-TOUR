History & Culture

A Timeline of Sovereignty

Ancient Foundations: From Lucy (Dinknesh) to the Aksumite Empire, Ethiopia has shaped human history. It was one of the first nations to adopt Christianity and Islam.

The Battle of Adwa (1896): A defining moment in global history where Ethiopia defeated a colonial power to maintain total independence.

Solomonic Line: A royal lineage tracing back to King Solomon and Queen of Sheba, lasting thousands of years.

Diplomatic Hub: Home to the African Union and a beacon of Pan-Africanism.
