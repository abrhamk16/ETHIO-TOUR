Culture & History

The Epic History of Ethiopia

Ancient Origins & Aksum: Ethiopia is home to the earliest ancestors of humanity (Lucy). By 100 AD, the Aksumite Empire became one of the four great powers of the world.

Medieval Sovereignty: During the Zagwe dynasty, the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela were built. Later, Gondar became the city of castles.

Modern Victory: Ethiopia is the only African nation never colonized. The Battle of Adwa in 1896 remains a global symbol of freedom.

20th Century: Emperor Haile Selassie I led the modernization of the state and the founding of the OAU (African Union).
